LCDB workflows
==============

**LCDB workflows** is a collection of `snakemake
<https://bitbucket.org/snakemake/snakemake/wiki/Home>`_ workflows combined with
supporting infrastructure to support best-practices, reproducible, highly
parallel processing of high-throughput sequencing data on a high-performance
computing cluster.
